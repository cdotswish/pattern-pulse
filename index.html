How to Play

Click on asteroids to destroy them before they reach the bottom!

Complete each level by destroying the required number of asteroids.

Each level gets progressively harder with:

  • Faster asteroids
  • More frequent spawns
  • More asteroids needed to progress

If any asteroid reaches the bottom, it's game over!
